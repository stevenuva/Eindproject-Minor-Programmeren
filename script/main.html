Agriculture and Population growth

How sustainable is the current population growth?

In 1799 demographer Thomas Malthus predicted that in the future there will come a day when there will not be enough food produced to feed everybody, and that too much land is being used for agriculture. This website will illustrate whether there are any indications that indicate that Malthus might be right and that overpopulation is or will be a real problem.
